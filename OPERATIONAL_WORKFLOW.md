## 2. Internal Record Keeping (The "Manual Backend")

**Important:** To build the analytics we need for Phase Gates, staff must record every 24 hours:
- Total Orders Received
- Total Revenue Generated
- Most Popular Item of the Day
- Any complaints or canceled orders

*Suggestion: Keep a simple Google Sheet or physical logbook linked to the WhatsApp business line.*

## 3. WhatsApp Business Features to Use
- **Quick Replies:** Save the messages above as shortcuts (e.g., `/greet`, `/confirm`).
- **Labels:** Use WhatsApp Labels to track order stages:
  - `New Order` (Blue)
  - `Prep / Cooking` (Yellow)
  - `Out for Delivery` (Purple)
  - `Completed / Paid` (Green)
